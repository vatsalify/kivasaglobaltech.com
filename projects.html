Case Study

Mehsana Railway Station

The Challenge

The existing lighting infrastructure at Mehsana Railway Station was outdated, energy-intensive, and provided insufficient visibility for passenger safety and comfort.

Our Solution

Kivasa Globaltech designed and executed a complete lighting overhaul using energy-efficient LED systems from Jaquar, covering platforms, concourse, parking, and outdoor areas.

The Result

Significantly improved visibility and aesthetics, reduced energy consumption, enhanced passenger experience, and compliance with modern railway infrastructure standards.
