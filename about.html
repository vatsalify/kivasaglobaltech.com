Our Story

Building India's Infrastructure, One Project at a Time

Kivasa Globaltech Pvt Ltd is a premium, infrastructure-focused solutions provider specializing in lighting and electrical systems for the nation's most critical projects. From railway stations to commercial complexes, from industrial facilities to residential towers, we deliver reliable, efficient, and high-quality electrical solutions.

Our brand speaks confidently, clearly, and practically. We focus on solutions, timelines, and results. With direct OEM partnerships and a commitment to authentic sourcing, we have built a reputation as the trusted go-to partner for contractors, infrastructure companies, procurement heads, real estate groups, and government contracting companies.

We understand the frustrations that plague the industry — delays, poor quality, unprofessionalism, lack of communication, over-commitment and under-delivery. That's exactly why Kivasa exists: to be the reliable, technical, and progressive partner that our clients deserve.

Our Mission

To empower Indian manufacturers by showcasing their exceptional products to a wider audience, both domestically and internationally. We strive to be a trusted partner, ensuring seamless market entry and growth for our clients.

Our Vision

To become the best Electrical Supply and Works company in the nation, setting new benchmarks in quality, reliability, and execution for infrastructure projects across all sectors.
